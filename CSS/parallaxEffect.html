Bear

Bears are large, stocky animals with non-retractable claws, shaggy fur, an excellent sense of smell, and short tails.

Leopard

Leopard belong to the big cat genus Panthera within the subfamily Pantherinae of the Felidae cat family.

Crocodile

Crocodiles are inhabitants of swamps, lakes, and rivers, although some species make their way to brackish water or to the sea.
